Our Process

How We Work

We follow a structured 3-step process to ensure your project runs smoothly from start to finish. Transparency and communication are key to our success.

1

Consultation & Concept

We meet to discuss your needs, style preferences, and budget. We then create a mood board and initial layout to visualize the direction.

2

Design & Development

Detailed drawings, 3D renderings, and material selection. We refine the design until it perfectly matches your vision.

3

Execution & Styling

We manage the construction, procurement, and installation. The final touch involves styling accessores to complete the look.
